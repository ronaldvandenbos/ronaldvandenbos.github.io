Welcome to Shell Learning portal

All personnel and contractors at each Shell business or operation shall be competent to perform their activities safely. Each Shell business or operation shall ensure that management and all relevant personnel receive regular training and coaching in hazard identification, risk assessment and control of all hazards in their workplace or areas of responsibility. Line managers shall be accountable for the development, implementation and monitoring of the effectiveness of this training.
