Welcome to the Red Cross Portal

We save lives every day and we help people get through crisis situations and disasters. In Denmark, we create communities and help those who are most vulnerable towards a better life. We help people on both sides of a conflict and in the most remote parts of the world. We do this regardless of nationality, religion or politics. The Red Cross is the world's largest humanitarian organisation with 17 million volunteers in 191 countries. We are part of your local community wherever you may live. With your help we will always be there when we are needed.
